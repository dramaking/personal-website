Design

At first glance, the website's design is minimalist, authentic and elegant. Dark colors from the background enhance the big pictures and bring out the true essence of the vibrant color used in Frances Hodgkins's paintings. On a closer look, the unique choice of font turns back the time and creates a vintage style which bring the users a truly immersive art viewing experience. Each page is designed with a distinct layer to demonstrate the flexibility of this type of minimalist design.
